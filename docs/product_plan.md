# Product Plan: Baan Bussaba

## 1. Product Vision
A "Digital Sanctuary" that serves as the primary promotional and information portal for Baan Bussaba. It must feel as calm and luxurious as walking into the property itself.

## 2. Target Audience
- **High-Net-Worth Individuals**: Looking for premium residential rentals or purchases.
- **Architectural Enthusiasts**: Interested in modern tropical design.
- **Corporate Partners**: Looking for premium venue spaces or long-term stays.

## 3. Key Pillars
- **Cinematic Experience**: Use of high-quality video backgrounds and GSAP-driven scroll reveals.
- **Organic Design**: A palette of Creamy Beige, Deep Mahogany, and Botanical Green.
- **Simplicity**: Minimalistic navigation focused on the visual beauty of the rooms and gardens.

## 4. User Experience Goals
- **The Wow Factor**: The Hero component must stop users in their tracks.
- **Seamless Flow**: Every scroll and transition should feel purposeful and smooth (no jank).
- **Responsive Luxury**: The experience must be equally premium on a 4K desktop and a mobile device.
